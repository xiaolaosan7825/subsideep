常见问题

Subsideep 需要联网吗？
不需要。所有声音和功能都在本地运行，无需网络连接。
为什么我听不到声音？
请检查：1) 手机静音开关是否关闭 2) 音量是否过低 3) 是否连接了蓝牙设备。Subsideep 推荐使用耳机以获得沉浸体验。
可以后台播放吗？
可以。锁定屏幕或切换到其他 App 后，声音会继续播放。
数据会传到服务器吗？
不会。你的睡眠记录、照片和笔记只保存在本地设备上，不上传任何服务器。

FAQ

Does Subsideep need internet?
No. All sounds and features run locally. No internet connection required.
I can't hear anything. What should I do?
Check: 1) The mute switch on your iPhone is off 2) Volume is turned up 3) No Bluetooth device is overriding output. Headphones are recommended for the best experience.
Can it play in the background?
Yes. Sound continues when you lock the screen or switch to another app.
Is my data uploaded?
No. Your sleep records, photos, and notes stay on your device only. No data is sent to any server.
